What are Hybrid Cars?

  • Hybrid cars are vehicles that are powered with an internal combustion engine that uses Gasoline and an electric motor that uses a battery.

Why would you buy a Hybrid car?

  • You will get less emissions as Hybrid cars emit less pollution than standard gasoline cars.

  • You will get more mileage per full tank as Hybrid cars use electric motor in city traffic which is charged by the gasoline engine.

  • You will get a more reliable car as Hybrid cars require less maintenance.
