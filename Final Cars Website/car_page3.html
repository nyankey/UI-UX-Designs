2020 HYBRID TOYOTA CAMRY

Exterior: The standard edition gets a prominent dark gray grille, automatic bi-LED combination headlights with integrated LED daytime running lights, LED combination taillights, color-keyed power side-view mirrors, and it rides on 16-inch steel wheels.

Interior: A seven-inch touchscreen is standard, inheriting last year's functionality in Apple CarPlay and Amazon Alexa, and boasting Bluetooth, SiriusXM, and a Wi-Fi connect mobile hotspot. But new for 2020, the Camry Hybrid is among the first Toyotas to be given Android Auto functionality.

Power and Safety: A 2.5-liter four-cylinder develops 176 horsepower and 163 lb-ft of torque, supplemented by an 88kW electric motor for a combined system output of 208 hp Continuous Variable Transmission with City/Hwy: 51/53 mpg. 2020 Camry comes with blind-spot monitoring with rear cross-traffic alert. Standard across the range, the Toyota Safety Sense P suite of pre-collision features includes pedestrian detection and automatic emergency braking as well as suite airbags.
