2020 Toyota Avalon Le Hybrid

Exterior: The luxurious Toyota Avalon is sleek in design offering 18-in alloy multi-spoke wheels, turn signal indicators in the side view mirrors, adjustable side view mirrors with standard blind spot monitors and rear cross traffic alerts, smart key auto open, push start. The hybrid Avalon offers radar cruise control, pedestrian detection, auto high beams, lane departure and steering assistance. It also has dynamic LED head/tail lamps and turn signals with smoked chrome bezels and a bird's eye view camera. The rear offers a backup camera and parking sonar..

Interior:The Toyota Avalon sleek design transfers to the interior offering a heated steering wheel, leather heated/cooled seats throughout lots of leg and head room, rear air vents, storage pockets on the back of the driver/passenger seats, combination cup holder armrest in the backseat, and child safety seat latches. The driver and passenger seats are both power with lumbar support. There are woodgrain accents throughout the interior of the vehicle 7-in multi- dimensional display with navigation, digital speedometer, frameless auto dimming mirror, sunroof, and safety connect. It has three USB inputs and a wireless charging bay. The trucks cargo space is very large with the option of laying the back seats down. The Avalon seat five passengers.​

Power and Safety: The Avalon offers city/highway/comb 43/43/43-MPG which has a 2.5 4-cylinder engine combined with electric motors that combines a net horsepower of 215. The Avalon has an electronically controlled continuously variable transmission-CVT which is very reliable and offers sport mode. The Toyota Avalon offers a very smooth and dependable ride and is a top-rated safety pick.​
