2019 Chevrolet Volt Hybrid

Exterior: The Chevrolet Volt exterior displays LED headlamps as well as LED accent strips at the bottom of the bumper. Behind the Chevrolet logo on the front of the vehicle there is a radar adapter cruise control sensor. The charging port is located on the front fender charging at 2.3 hours. At about 148 inches long the compact Volt offers a lift back for cargo practicality. The elegantly designed rear end displays dark accents on the back end which really brings out the color and design of the vehicle. The tale lamps are an incandescent combination of both turn and reverse signals. The rear end includes parking sensors with parking assistance capability.

Interior: The Chevrolet Volt front seat offers tilt/telescopic steering column and a two-way adjustable driver seat, manual adjustable passenger seats. The back seat offers 76.8 legroom and 35.8 headroom dimensions with overall heated seats. The dashboard has chrome accents, a slot style glove compartment, center channel speaker, touchscreen infotainment system which includes Apple CarPlay/Android Auto and OnStar, USB/auxiliary inputs and a 12-volt port. The cargo area is large with the option to flip down the back seats for more space. The Chevrolet Volt seats five passengers.

Power and Safety: The Volt has a Planetary Power Split 2-motor EV mode hybrid system with two electric motor units MGA- 64HP and MGB- 116HP totaling 149HP and 264lb-ft. The Volt can drive with both options or one coming in at city/highway/comb 113/99/106-MPGe EV or 43/42/42-MPG Hybrid. It can also operate as a serial or parallel hybrid which improves overall efficiency. The 2019 Chevrolet Volt is a top-rated safety pick.
